Aviation & Maintenance Systems

Flight Maintenance Platform

What I Built: A complete system for aircraft maintenance. It includes digital checklists (no more paper), a tracker for all the maintenance work, and a tool to manage all the equipment. Technicians can use it offline, and everything syncs up when they go back online.

Real Results: Maintenance teams cut down on paperwork by 80%. Checklists are way faster to find. Everything's recorded automatically with timestamps. The system meets all the compliance requirements for aviation.
