Aviation & Maintenance Systems

Aircraft Maintenance, Aerospace, Compliance Ready

What I Do:

I build systems for maintenance teams in aviation. These are apps and tools that help mechanics, inspectors, and engineers do their jobs better – with less paperwork and zero room for error.

The Systems:

  • Maintenance tracking – Keep complete records of what's been fixed, when, and by who. Everything's logged automatically
  • Digital checklists – Instead of paper, technicians use tablets. Works offline, and can export to PDF automatically
  • Tool inventory – Track all your tools and equipment. Know when they need calibration and who's using them
  • Reports and compliance – Auto-generate reports for audits. Everything logged with timestamps
  • Field ready – Apps work on tablets, handle poor wifi, and sync up when you're online again

Compliance Support:

  • Built for FAA rules
  • Records stay forever (regulatory requirement)
  • Complete audit trails
  • Digital signatures where needed
